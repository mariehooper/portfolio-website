Bio

I like building web things that people love to use.

In my career so far, I've been a project manager, designer, front-end developer, and JavaScript instructor. I have led teams of designers and developers and love helping others discover how exciting it is to use technology solve problems.

I have experience building production React and Vue.js apps using both REST and GraphQL APIs, utilizing CI/CD pipelines, writing automated tests, and using just about every styling approach you can think of.

I'm originally from Tampa, Florida and currently live in Ann Arbor, Michigan. When I'm not glued to my MacBook, you can find me hiking, biking, playing rec sports, or yelling at college football games.
